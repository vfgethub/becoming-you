A Creative Development Journey

Discover the path to self-awareness, creativity, and purpose — a journey that inspires growth from within.

In a rapidly changing world, the greatest journey is not outward — but inward. Becoming You: A Creative Development Journey is more than a book. It's a movement to nurture creativity, purpose, and emotional intelligence among the next generation of Filipinos.

Created by Jane Catherine Rojo-Tiu, a visionary lawyer and educator, this initiative reimagines how personal growth and education can unite — shaping a future where every student learns not just what to think, but who they are meant to become.

Our Vision for Education

Our vision is for Becoming You to evolve into a national curriculum subject — a learning journey that helps students discover their strengths, define their purpose, and design meaningful futures.

Through reflection, creativity, and guided exercises, the program aims to build a new generation of Filipinos who are grounded in self-awareness, guided by integrity, and driven by vision.

Purpose & Vision

This project was born from a deep belief that every young person deserves the opportunity to discover their unique path and purpose before stepping into the world.

We believe that education should nurture not just knowledge, but wisdom—not just skills, but self-awareness. This book is the beginning of a larger vision to transform how Filipino youth are prepared for life.

Jane Catherine Rojo-Tiu

Founder & Author

Jane is a visionary educator and advocate for life-purpose integration in Philippine education. Her work bridges personal transformation with systemic change.

The Book

Becoming You: A Creative Development Journey is a reflective and interactive guide that helps young Filipinos navigate the most important questions of their lives: Who am I? What do I value? What kind of life do I want to create?

Inspired by Joseph Campbell's Hero's Journey and Debbie Millman's Life Design Plan, this book offers a structured yet deeply personal framework for self-discovery and intentional living.

Self-Discovery

Guided exercises to explore identity, values, and authentic self-expression.

Life Design

Practical tools to envision and plan a meaningful, purpose-driven future.

Reflective Journey

A thoughtful companion that grows with you through life's transitions.

Vision & Educational Framework
